rolloverDay()

The first day of the billing cycle. My billing cycle rollover date is the eleventh. If I‘ve downloaded up to 100% of my bandwidth limit and have to stop, on the 11th I can start downloading again because the 11th of every month is the first day of my new billing cycle. So, I‘d enter 11. You need to add a rolloverDay for each interface, and it must be in the for of a hash such as {:eth0=>11}.
